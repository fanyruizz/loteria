Melon


Frase

El melón y sus aromas, un pedazo me has de dar. The melon and its aromas, you have to give me a piece.

Significado

The melon is card number 11 in the Mexican Lottery, The melon card in the Mexican Lottery is a symbol of freshness, abundance and connection with nature. Its meaning in the game enriches the experience, reminding players of the importance of enjoying joyful moments and valuing cultural traditions.
