Frase

El auxilio de San Luis, que le llaman el nopal. The help of Saint Louis, They call it the nopal.

Significado

The nopal is card number 39 in the Mexican Lottery, The nopal card in the Mexican Lottery is a symbol of cultural identity, resilience and connection with nature. Its meaning in the game enriches the experience, reminding players of the importance of heritage and adaptability in their lives.
