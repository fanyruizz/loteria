Frase

La bota rechina, la bota del general. The boot squeaks, the general's boot.

Significado

The boot is card number 22 in the Mexican Lottery, The boot card in the Mexican Lottery is a symbol of adventure, tradition and camaraderie. Its meaning in the game enriches the experience, reminding players of the importance of exploring, celebrating and staying strong in the face of challenges.
