Significado

The deer is card number 45 in the Mexican Lottery, The deer card in the Mexican Lottery is a symbol of nature, spirituality and tradition. Its meaning in the game enriches the experience, reminding players of the importance of freedom, connection with the environment and the value of cultural traditions.
